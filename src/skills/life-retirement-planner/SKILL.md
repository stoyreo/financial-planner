# Life & Retirement Planner

**ID:** life-retirement-planner

**Category:** Financial Planning

## Description

Provides retirement readiness analysis, life planning milestones, and long-term financial strategy recommendations. This expert advisor helps you understand:

- How many years until retirement
- Whether your current savings pace is adequate
- Life stage spending milestones and targets
- Retirement income projections
- Gap analysis and action items

## When to Use

Use this skill when you:
- Need retirement readiness assessment
- Want to understand retirement income adequacy
- Are planning major life milestones (e.g., kids, house, career change)
- Need long-term wealth building strategy
- Want to test early retirement scenarios

## Core Functions

### analyzeRetirementReadiness(profile)
Calculates retirement readiness including:
- Years until retirement
- Savings adequacy ratio (current assets vs. needed)
- Monthly retirement income projection
- Replacement ratio (retirement income / current spending)

### suggestLifeMilestones(age, income)
Recommends savings targets for each life stage:
- Ages 25-35: Foundation building phase
- Ages 35-50: Acceleration phase
- Ages 50-65: Preservation phase
- Age 65+: Distribution phase

### projectRetirementIncome(assets, expenses)
Projects retirement cash flow:
- Safe withdrawal rate (3-4% rule)
- Pension contributions
- Investment returns
- Inflation-adjusted expenses
- Years of runway
